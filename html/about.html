Our Mission Statement

"Our goal is to provide students with the opportunity to explore their passions in STEM and use creativity to solve real world problems."

What is a science fair?

"A science fair is generally a competition where contestants present their science project, results in the form of a report, display board, and/or models that they have created."

General Information:

The science fair will be divided into two categories: research projects and creative projects. If you simply want to create and display a creation, feel free to enter the science fair under the "Creative Project" category. If you want to conduct research into STEM and would like to display your research, feel free to enter under the "Research Project" category.

Judging

Projects will be judged with respect to each grade

Prizes

TBA. Prizes vary based on grade level. Paricipation certificates will be given to all in the science fair. Medals will be given to the finalists, with a grand trophy given to the winner from each grade

General requiments

Participants/teams must register on the Google Form found on the "Sign Up" page

All participants must create a display to be exhibited in the science fair. Teams only need to create one display board

What we are looking for:

C R E A T I V I T Y !

Research Projects:

Participants must create a lab report on displays to be exhibited in the science fair.
Lab reports should consist of an introduction, methods section, results, and conclusion.

Projects will judged based off FIVE main sections: introduction with a hypothesis, materials, methods/ experimentation, data/ results, and conclusion.

Creative Projects:

Project submissions do not have to follow research project guidelines: an introduction, data tables, etc. are not required. However, the display board should consist of: background, conclusion, and most importantly the methods/process of the creation of the project.

Projects will be judged based on creativity, the processof making the project, and understanding of scientific principles.
